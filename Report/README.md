| Section | Content |

| # | Finding |

## 📄 File 2 — Retention Playbook

### `RETENTION_PLAYBOOK.pdf`

> A 4-page strategic playbook translating the full analysis into actionable business recommendations — including a 90-day execution roadmap, ideal customer profile, and final strategic recommendation.

#### Page Breakdown

**Page 1 — Executive Context**
- Project objective and strategic focus
- Key insight: customer value is driven more by loyalty behavior than revenue alone

**Page 2 — Strategic Insights & Business Impact**
- Business Insight vs Recommended Action table
- Expected business outcomes
- Key strategic insight with segment percentages
- What this means for the business (Continue / Reduce / Adopt framework)

**Page 3 — Strategic Action Framework**
- Priority 1: Protect Premium Loyalists
- Priority 2: Reduce Discount Dependency
- Priority 3: Improve Customer Intelligence
- Priority 4: Invest In High-Value Regions
- Strategic insight summary + success metrics

**Page 4 — 90-Day Promo Sunset Plan**
- Month 1: Reduce Blanket Discounting
- Month 2: Replace Discounts With Value
- Month 3: Strategic Promotion Model
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) characteristics
- Final Recommendation
- Key Takeaway

#### 90-Day Roadmap Summary

| Month | Focus | Expected Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Month 1 | Reduce blanket discounting by 20% | Initial reduction in discount dependency |
| Month 2 | Replace discounts with VIP benefits and referral incentives | Improved engagement and repeat purchase motivation |
| Month 3 | Restrict discounts to seasonal campaigns only | Lower dependency, improved profitability, stronger retention |

#### Ideal Customer Profile

```
✔  Low Promotional Dependency
✔  High Behavioral Loyalty
✔  Frequent Repeat Purchases
✔  Consistent Spending Patterns
✔  Multi-Category Engagement
✔  Strong Purchase Intensity
✔  Belongs to Premium Loyalist Segment
```
